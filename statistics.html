Tree Cover In Nyeri

As of 2018, 38.03% of Nyeri was tree cover. The top 2 regions represent 64% of all tree cover. Othaya had the most relative tree cover at 61% compared to an average of 45%.

Tree Cover Loss In Nyeri

From 2001 to 2016, Nyeri lost 3.96kha of tree cover, equivalent to a 2.3% decrease since 2000.

In Nyeri, the top 6 regions were responsible for 100% of all tree cover loss between 2001 and 2016. Tetu had the most relative tree cover loss at 2.7% compared to an average of 2.1%.
